Privacy Policy

App: Water Sort  •  Developer: SM Games  •  Last updated: 16 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SM Games ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with the mobile game Water Sort (the "App"). By installing or using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

The App itself does not require you to create an account and does not ask you to provide personal information such as your name, email, or address. However, the App uses third-party services that may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Advertising identifiers (e.g. Google Advertising ID) and device information such as device model, operating system version, language, and approximate location (derived from IP address).
  • Usage and diagnostic data related to ad delivery and app performance.
  • Purchase information when you buy the in-app "Remove Interstitial Ads" product (processed by Google Play; we do not receive or store your payment details).

Your game progress (such as your current level and settings) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, which have their own privacy policies governing how they handle data:

  • Google AdMob (advertising) — https://policies.google.com/privacy

  • Unity (Unity Technologies) (game engine and in-app purchasing) — https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

3. How the Information Is Used

Information collected by these third-party services is used to:

  • Display advertisements, including personalized or non-personalized ads depending on your consent and region;
  • Process in-app purchases;
  • Measure ad performance and detect invalid or fraudulent activity;
  • Maintain and improve the App's stability and performance.

4. Advertising and Your Consent

The App shows advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Where required by law (for example, for users in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and other applicable regions), the App presents a consent request before serving personalized ads, using Google's User Messaging Platform. You can change or withdraw your consent at any time from within the App's Settings menu ("Privacy & Ads").

You can also reset or limit ad personalization through your device settings (Google Advertising ID controls).

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. Your Rights (GDPR, India DPDP, and Other Laws)

Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict its processing, and to withdraw consent. Because we do not directly collect or store personal data that identifies you, most such requests are handled by the third-party services listed above under their respective policies. For requests directed to us, please use the contact details below.

For users in India, this policy is intended to align with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act. For users in the EEA/UK, processing of advertising data is based on your consent as collected in-app.

7. Data Retention

We do not maintain servers that store your personal data. Locally stored game data remains on your device until you delete the App or clear its data. Data handled by third-party services is retained according to their own retention policies.

8. Security

We rely on the security measures of the third-party platforms (Google, Unity) that process data in connection with the App. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
